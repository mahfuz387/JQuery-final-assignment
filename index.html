About Me

I am a website designer based in Tunbridge Wells, Kent. I specialise in website design, 3D visualisation and identity.

I create beautiful, usable, professional websites using best practice accessibility and the latest W3C web standards guidelines, resulting in semantic and seo friendly XHTML and CSS. All my websites are lovingly hand coded.For building websites my weapons of choice are Photoshop and Fireworks for creating layouts, then Coda to produce clean semantic XHTML and CSS. I code to XHTML strict 1. I also use Flash for animations and sometimes full Flash sites. My CMS of choice are Expression Engine and Wordpress
